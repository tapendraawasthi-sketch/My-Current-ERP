2. HOW ADVANCED IT SHOULD BE (HONEST BAR)
======================================================================

‚ÄúAdvanced‚Äù for MokXya means these bars, in order:

BAR A ‚Äî SAFE
  Wrong mutation, cross-tenant leak, or false legal current-law claim is blocked.
  Ask Mode cannot mutate. NL ‚Äúyes/huncha‚Äù alone cannot post.

  English + Devanagari + Romanized + code-mix produce stable slots for supported
  events. Number/date/money roles do not treat ‚Äú5 months‚Äù as NPR 5.
  New topics do not silently merge into stale drafts.

BAR C ‚Äî USEFUL FOR BOOKKEEPING
  Supported purchase/sales/payment-class events go draft ‚Üí preview ‚Üí confirm ‚Üí
  receipt on one documented write authority, with idempotency and audit.

  No-answer when evidence is missing. Citations entail claims. Legal/tax
  currentness is never invented. Prod retrieval does not depend on a local-only
  Ollama/Chroma stack the cloud never runs.

  One capability matrix row can be PRODUCTION_APPROVED with monitoring,
  rollback, and disclosed limitations ‚Äî not ‚Äúall of AI is live.‚Äù

BAR F ‚Äî PROFESSIONAL EXTENSIONS (LATER)
  Speech, private documents, CA workpapers, compliance calendars ‚Äî only after
  Bars A‚ÄìE for the launch slice. Track I in the master file remains future work;
  current MAI-50‚Ä¶53 engineering stubs are *not* those capabilities.
