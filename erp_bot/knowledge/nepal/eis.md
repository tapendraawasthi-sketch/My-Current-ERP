# EIS — Electronic Invoice System (Nepal)

> IRD mandates electronic invoicing for VAT-registered businesses. Rules evolve — verify at https://ird.gov.np.

## What is EIS?

**Electronic Invoice System (EIS)** is IRD's platform for issuing, validating, and reporting VAT invoices digitally. Replaces or supplements paper tax invoices for registered taxpayers.

## Who must use EIS?

VAT-registered businesses as notified by IRD circulars. Large taxpayers and specified sectors are phased in first.

## Key requirements

- Issue invoices through IRD-approved billing software or ERP integration
- Each invoice carries a unique **IRD invoice number** and QR validation
- Real-time or batch sync to IRD servers per technical specification
- Maintain audit trail matching physical dispatch and accounting entries

## Accounting impact

Sales invoices posted in ERP must match EIS-submitted amounts. VAT output tax is reported from EIS-registered invoice totals in monthly VAT return (Form 13).

## Penalties

Non-compliance (missing EIS invoice, mismatch with return) may attract fines and disallowance of input credit on purchases.
