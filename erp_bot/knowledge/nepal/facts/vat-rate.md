# VAT Standard Rate (Nepal)

**Standard VAT rate: 13%** on most taxable goods and services in Nepal.

Single-rate system. VAT-inclusive price formula: `VAT = (inclusive × 13) ÷ 113`.

Registration thresholds (annual turnover): goods Rs. 50 lakh; services Rs. 20 lakh.

Verify current IRD rules at https://ird.gov.np before filing.
