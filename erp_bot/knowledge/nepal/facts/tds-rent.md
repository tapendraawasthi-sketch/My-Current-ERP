# TDS on Rent (Nepal)

House/building/land/vehicle rent TDS (FY 2080/81 reference):

| Payee | PAN holder | Non-PAN |
|-------|------------|---------|
| Rent | 10% | 15% |

Residential rent below Rs. 25,000/month may be exempt from TDS. Deduct at payment, deposit via IRD within 25 days.

Verify current rates at https://ird.gov.np.
