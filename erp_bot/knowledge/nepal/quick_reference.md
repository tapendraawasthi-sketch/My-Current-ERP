# Nepal Accounting & Tax — Quick Reference (Orbix RAG)

> One-page facts for fast retrieval. Verify rates with IRD before filing.

## VAT

| Item | Value |
|------|-------|
| Standard rate | **13%** |
| Goods registration threshold | Rs. 50,00,000 / year |
| Services registration threshold | Rs. 20,00,000 / year |
| Zero-rated | Exports, intl transport, duty-free sales |
| Exempt (no input credit) | Basic food grains, fresh veg/meat, salt, kerosene (domestic), seeds, fertilizers, govt hospital services, school fees, books |

## TDS (withholding)

| Payment type | Typical rate |
|--------------|--------------|
| Rent (building/land) | 10% |
| Professional / technical fee | 15% |
| Dividend | 5% |
| Interest | 5% |
| Contract / construction | 1.5% |
| Salary | Per slab (employer withholds) |

Always check IRD schedule for current thresholds and exemptions.

## SSF (Social Security Fund)

- Employer + employee contributions on salary (rates set by SSF Act).
- Registration mandatory for covered employers.
- Separate from income tax withholding.

## Income tax (business)

- Fiscal year: **Shrawan 1 – Ashadh end** (Nepal BS calendar).
- Corporate / business income: progressive slabs per Finance Act.
- Advance tax: quarterly instalments for many businesses.

## Fiscal year

| BS month | AD approx | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| Shrawan–Ashadh | Jul–Jul | Nepal FY |
| Year-end close | Ashadh 32 / end Ashadh | Trial balance, P&L |

## Double-entry basics

| Event | Debit | Credit |
|-------|-------|--------|
| Cash sale | Cash/Bank | Sales |
| Credit sale to customer | Accounts Receivable | Sales |
| Purchase on credit | Purchases/Inventory | Accounts Payable |
| Pay supplier | Accounts Payable | Cash/Bank |
| Salary paid | Salary expense | Cash/Bank (+ TDS payable if withheld) |
| VAT on sale | — | Output VAT 13% (credit Sales net of VAT per policy) |

**Rule:** Total Debits = Total Credits on every voucher.

## Gratuity (Nepal labour)

- Payable after qualifying service period under Labour Act.
- Provision: Dr Gratuity expense, Cr Gratuity payable.
- Rate/f formula per employment contract and law.
