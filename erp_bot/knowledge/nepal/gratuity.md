# Nepal Gratuity & Employee Benefits

> ⚠️ **DISCLAIMER**: Labor laws and benefit rates change. Verify current rules with the Department of Labor and Social Security Fund.

## Gratuity

### What is Gratuity?

Gratuity is a statutory benefit paid to employees upon separation from employment (resignation, retirement, or termination) as recognition for their service.

### Who is Eligible?

- Employees who have completed **minimum 3 years** of continuous service
- Applies to permanent employees in formal sector
- Part-time and contract workers may have different rules

### Gratuity Rates (Non-SSF Members)

For organizations NOT under SSF:

| Service Period | Gratuity Rate |
|----------------|---------------|
| 3-7 years | 50% of last drawn basic salary × years |
| 7-15 years | 67% of last drawn basic salary × years |
| 15-20 years | 81% of last drawn basic salary × years |
| 20+ years | 100% of last drawn basic salary × years |

### Gratuity Under SSF

For SSF members, gratuity is covered under the employer's **8.33%** monthly contribution. No separate gratuity payment at retirement.

### Gratuity Calculation Example

**Employee Details:**
- Last Basic Salary: Rs. 40,000
- Service Period: 10 years
- Not under SSF

**Calculation:**
```
Gratuity Rate (7-15 years) = 67%
Gratuity = 40,000 × 67% × 10 = Rs. 2,68,000
```

### Journal Entry for Gratuity Payment

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Gratuity Expense | 2,68,000 | - |
| Bank / Cash | - | 2,68,000 |

Or if accrued over time:

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Gratuity Payable | 2,68,000 | - |
| Bank / Cash | - | 2,68,000 |

### Monthly Gratuity Accrual (Non-SSF)

If not under SSF, companies should accrue gratuity monthly:

**Monthly Accrual = (Basic Salary × Expected Rate) ÷ 12**

Example: Rs. 40,000 basic, expected 10 years service (67% rate)

```
Annual Gratuity Accrual = 40,000 × 67% = 26,800
Monthly Accrual = 26,800 ÷ 12 = Rs. 2,233
```

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Gratuity Expense | 2,233 | - |
| Gratuity Provision | - | 2,233 |

## Provident Fund (PF)

### Non-SSF Organizations

| Contributor | Rate |
|-------------|------|
| Employee | 10% of basic |
| Employer | 10% of basic |
| **Total** | **20%** |

### SSF Organizations

PF is included in SSF contributions (10% employee + 10% employer = 20% total PF component).

### PF Withdrawal

- **Full withdrawal**: On retirement, resignation after 5+ years
- **Partial withdrawal**: For housing, medical emergency (with conditions)

## CIT (Citizen Investment Trust)

### Contribution Limits

| Contributor | Maximum Deductible |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Employee | Rs. 3,00,000/year (combined PF + CIT) |
| Employer | No maximum (fully deductible as expense) |

### Tax Benefit

Employee's CIT contribution is deductible from taxable income up to the limit.

## Leave Encashment

### Annual Leave (Bida)

| Leave Type | Entitlement | Encashment |
|------------|------------|------------|
| Annual Leave | 18 days/year | Up to 60 days accumulated |
| Sick Leave | 12 days/year | Usually not encashable |
| Public Holidays | As per calendar | Not applicable |

### Leave Encashment Calculation

**Encashment Rate = Basic Salary ÷ 30 days**

Example: 30 days leave encashment, Basic Rs. 40,000
```
Daily Rate = 40,000 ÷ 30 = Rs. 1,333
Leave Encashment = 30 × 1,333 = Rs. 40,000
```

### Journal Entry for Leave Encashment

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Leave Encashment Expense | 40,000 | - |
| TDS Payable (if applicable) | - | [TDS Amount] |
| Bank / Cash | - | [Net Amount] |

## Medical Benefits

### Under SSF

Medical is covered under employer's 1% contribution.

### Non-SSF Organizations

Many organizations provide:
- Medical allowance (fixed monthly)
- Medical reimbursement (actual expenses up to limit)
- Group health insurance

### Medical Allowance Entry

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Medical Allowance Expense | [Amount] | - |
| Salary Payable / Cash | - | [Amount] |

## Dashain Bonus (Festival Allowance)

### Entitlement

All employees who have completed **1 year** of service are entitled to Dashain bonus.

### Amount

**One month's basic salary** (or pro-rata if less than 1 year)

### Due Date

Must be paid **before Dashain** (typically Ashwin/Kartik)

### Journal Entry

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Dashain Bonus Expense | [Amount] | - |
| TDS Payable | - | [TDS if any] |
| Bonus Payable / Bank | - | [Net Amount] |

## Maternity Benefit

### Entitlement

- **60 days** paid maternity leave
- Additional 15 days in case of complications
- Applicable to female employees with 1+ year service

### Rate

Full salary during leave period (basic + allowances)
