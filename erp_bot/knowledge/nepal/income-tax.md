# Nepal Income Tax (Overview)

> Verify current slabs and thresholds with IRD at https://ird.gov.np.

## Individual Income Tax (FY 2080/81 reference)

Progressive slabs apply to **assessable income** after deductions.

| Income slab (annual) | Rate |
|---------------------|------|
| Up to Rs. 5,00,000 | 1% |
| Rs. 5,00,001 – 7,00,000 | 10% on excess over 5L |
| Rs. 7,00,001 – 10,00,000 | 20% on excess over 7L |
| Rs. 10,00,001 – 20,00,000 | 30% on excess over 10L |
| Above Rs. 20,00,000 | 36% on excess over 20L |

Married couples may file jointly with adjusted slabs per IRD rules.

## Corporate Income Tax

Standard corporate rate is **25%** on net taxable income for most companies. Banks, insurance, and petroleum have sector-specific rates.

## Advance Tax

Quarterly advance tax installments based on prior-year liability or estimated current-year income.

## TDS as Advance Tax

TDS deducted on payments is creditable against final income tax liability of the payee.
