# Nepal Fiscal Year & BS/AD Calendar

> ⚠️ **DISCLAIMER**: Verify fiscal year deadlines and tax filing dates with IRD for the current year.

## Nepal Fiscal Year

Nepal's fiscal year runs from **Shrawan 1 to Ashad End** (mid-July to mid-July).

| Fiscal Year (BS) | Start Date (AD) | End Date (AD) |
|-----------------|-----------------|---------------|
| 2078/79 | Jul 16, 2021 | Jul 16, 2022 |
| 2079/80 | Jul 17, 2022 | Jul 15, 2023 |
| 2080/81 | Jul 16, 2023 | Jul 15, 2024 |
| 2081/82 | Jul 16, 2024 | Jul 14, 2025 |
| 2082/83 | Jul 15, 2025 | Jul 15, 2026 |

## Bikram Sambat (BS) Months

| BS Month | Typical AD Dates | Days |
|----------|-----------------|------|
| 1. Baishakh | Apr 14 - May 14 | 30-31 |
| 2. Jestha | May 15 - Jun 14 | 31-32 |
| 3. Ashad | Jun 15 - Jul 15 | 31-32 |
| 4. Shrawan | Jul 16 - Aug 16 | 31-32 |
| 5. Bhadra | Aug 17 - Sep 16 | 31-32 |
| 6. Ashwin | Sep 17 - Oct 17 | 30-31 |
| 7. Kartik | Oct 18 - Nov 16 | 29-30 |
| 8. Mangsir | Nov 17 - Dec 15 | 29-30 |
| 9. Poush | Dec 16 - Jan 14 | 29-30 |
| 10. Magh | Jan 15 - Feb 12 | 29-30 |
| 11. Falgun | Feb 13 - Mar 14 | 29-30 |
| 12. Chaitra | Mar 15 - Apr 13 | 30-31 |

Note: BS months have variable days (29-32) unlike AD months.

## Key Tax Deadlines

### Advance Tax (Estimated Tax)

| Installment | Due Date (BS) | Approximate AD |
|-------------|---------------|----------------|
| 1st Installment (40%) | Poush End | Mid-January |
| 2nd Installment (70%) | Chaitra End | Mid-April |
| 3rd Installment (100%) | Ashad End | Mid-July |

### VAT Returns

| Return | Due Date |
|--------|----------|
| Monthly VAT | 25th of following BS month |
| Annual Reconciliation | 3 months after Ashad End |

### TDS Returns

| Return | Due Date |
|--------|----------|
| Monthly TDS | 25th of following BS month |
| Annual TDS | 3 months after Ashad End |

### Income Tax Returns

| Taxpayer Type | Filing Deadline |
|---------------|----------------|
| Individual (Salary Only) | Kartik End (~ Nov 16) |
| Individual (Business) | Ashwin End (~ Oct 17) |
| Company/Firm | Ashwin End (~ Oct 17) |
| Extended (with approval) | Poush End (~ Jan 14) |

### SSF Deposit

| Deadline | Details |
|----------|---------|
| Monthly | 15th of following BS month |

## BS to AD Conversion Formula

The relationship between BS and AD years is approximately:
- **BS Year = AD Year + 56 or 57** (depending on month)
- Before Baishakh: BS = AD + 57
- After Baishakh: BS = AD + 56

| 2024 | 2081 |

## Public Holidays Affecting Deadlines

If a deadline falls on a public holiday:
- **VAT/TDS**: Next working day
- **Income Tax**: No automatic extension (apply for extension)

Major holidays that may affect deadlines:
- Dashain (Ashwin/Kartik) - ~15 days
- Tihar (Kartik) - ~5 days
- Holi (Falgun) - 1 day
- Nepal New Year (Baishakh 1)

## Financial Statement Dates

| Statement | Reference Date |
|-----------|---------------|
| Balance Sheet | Ashad End (~ Jul 15) |
| Income Statement | Shrawan 1 to Ashad End |
| Trial Balance | Any BS month end |

## Depreciation Periods

Depreciation is calculated for the full BS fiscal year:
- Assets purchased before Poush End: Full year depreciation
- Assets purchased after Poush End: Half year depreciation (some cases)

## Accounting Period Best Practices

1. **Opening Balance**: Enter as of Shrawan 1
2. **Trial Balance**: Generate monthly at each BS month end
3. **VAT Settlement**: Monthly, by 25th
4. **Bank Reconciliation**: Monthly at BS month end
5. **Year-End Closing**: Ashad End
