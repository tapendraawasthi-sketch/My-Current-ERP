# Double-Entry Accounting Rules for Nepal

> ⚠️ **DISCLAIMER**: These are standard accounting principles. For complex transactions, consult a Chartered Accountant.

## The Golden Rules of Accounting

### For Real Accounts (Assets)
- **Debit** what comes IN
- **Credit** what goes OUT

### For Personal Accounts (Debtors/Creditors)
- **Debit** the receiver
- **Credit** the giver

### For Nominal Accounts (Income/Expense)
- **Debit** all expenses and losses
- **Credit** all incomes and gains

## Account Types and Normal Balances

| Account Type | Normal Balance | Increases By | Decreases By |
|--------------|---------------|--------------|--------------|
| **Assets** | Debit | Debit | Credit |
| **Liabilities** | Credit | Credit | Debit |
| **Capital/Equity** | Credit | Credit | Debit |
| **Income/Revenue** | Credit | Credit | Debit |
| **Expenses** | Debit | Debit | Credit |

## Common Transaction Journal Entries

### 1. Cash Sales

**Transaction**: Sold goods for Rs. 10,000 cash

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Cash / Bank | 10,000 | - |
| Sales Revenue | - | 10,000 |

### 2. Credit Sales

**Transaction**: Sold goods to Ram on credit for Rs. 15,000

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Accounts Receivable (Ram) | 15,000 | - |
| Sales Revenue | - | 15,000 |

### 3. Cash Purchase

**Transaction**: Purchased goods for Rs. 8,000 cash

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Purchases / Inventory | 8,000 | - |
| Cash / Bank | - | 8,000 |

### 4. Credit Purchase

**Transaction**: Purchased goods from Shyam on credit for Rs. 12,000

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Purchases / Inventory | 12,000 | - |
| Accounts Payable (Shyam) | - | 12,000 |

### 5. Receipt from Debtor

**Transaction**: Received Rs. 15,000 from Ram (debtor)

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Cash / Bank | 15,000 | - |
| Accounts Receivable (Ram) | - | 15,000 |

### 6. Payment to Creditor

**Transaction**: Paid Rs. 12,000 to Shyam (creditor)

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Accounts Payable (Shyam) | 12,000 | - |
| Cash / Bank | - | 12,000 |

### 7. Expense Payment

**Transaction**: Paid rent Rs. 20,000

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Rent Expense | 20,000 | - |
| Cash / Bank | - | 20,000 |

### 8. Salary Payment (with SSF & TDS)

**Transaction**: Paid salary Rs. 50,000 with SSF 11% and TDS 1%

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Salary Expense | 50,000 | - |
| SSF Expense (Employer 20%) | 10,000 | - |
| SSF Payable (Employee 11%) | - | 5,500 |
| SSF Payable (Employer 20%) | - | 10,000 |
| TDS Payable | - | 500 |
| Salary Payable / Bank | - | 44,000 |

### 9. Sales with VAT

**Transaction**: Sold goods for Rs. 10,000 + 13% VAT

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Accounts Receivable / Cash | 11,300 | - |
| Sales Revenue | - | 10,000 |
| VAT Payable | - | 1,300 |

### 10. Purchase with VAT

**Transaction**: Purchased goods for Rs. 8,000 + 13% VAT

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Purchases / Inventory | 8,000 | - |
| VAT Receivable (Input) | 1,040 | - |
| Accounts Payable / Cash | - | 9,040 |

### 11. Fixed Asset Purchase

**Transaction**: Bought computer for Rs. 80,000

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Computer (Fixed Asset) | 80,000 | - |
| Cash / Bank | - | 80,000 |

### 12. Depreciation Entry

**Transaction**: Annual depreciation on computer @ 25%

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Depreciation Expense | 20,000 | - |
| Accumulated Depreciation - Computer | - | 20,000 |

### 13. Loan Received

**Transaction**: Received bank loan Rs. 5,00,000

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Bank | 5,00,000 | - |
| Bank Loan | - | 5,00,000 |

### 14. Interest Payment

**Transaction**: Paid loan interest Rs. 4,000

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Interest Expense | 4,000 | - |
| Bank | - | 4,000 |

### 15. Owner's Capital Investment

**Transaction**: Owner invested Rs. 10,00,000 in business

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Bank / Cash | 10,00,000 | - |
| Owner's Capital | - | 10,00,000 |

### 16. Owner's Drawing

**Transaction**: Owner withdrew Rs. 25,000 for personal use

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Drawings | 25,000 | - |
| Cash / Bank | - | 25,000 |

### 17. Sales Return (by customer)

**Transaction**: Customer returned goods worth Rs. 2,000

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Sales Return | 2,000 | - |
| Accounts Receivable / Cash | - | 2,000 |

### 18. Purchase Return (to supplier)

**Transaction**: Returned goods to supplier worth Rs. 1,500

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Accounts Payable / Cash | 1,500 | - |
| Purchase Return | - | 1,500 |

### 19. Discount Allowed (to customer)

**Transaction**: Allowed Rs. 500 discount to customer on settlement

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Cash / Bank | 9,500 | - |
| Discount Allowed | 500 | - |
| Accounts Receivable | - | 10,000 |

### 20. Discount Received (from supplier)

**Transaction**: Received Rs. 300 discount from supplier on payment

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Accounts Payable | 10,000 | - |
| Cash / Bank | - | 9,700 |
| Discount Received | - | 300 |

## Contra Entries

**Transaction**: Withdrew Rs. 50,000 cash from bank

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Cash | 50,000 | - |
| Bank | - | 50,000 |

## Closing Entries (Year End)

### Close Income Accounts

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Sales Revenue | [Total Sales] | - |
| Other Income | [Total] | - |
| Income Summary / P&L | - | [Total Income] |

### Close Expense Accounts

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Income Summary / P&L | [Total Expenses] | - |
| Purchases | - | [Total] |
| Salaries | - | [Total] |
| Rent | - | [Total] |
| (other expenses...) | - | [Total] |

### Transfer Profit/Loss to Capital

**If Profit:**

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Income Summary / P&L | [Net Profit] | - |
| Owner's Capital / Retained Earnings | - | [Net Profit] |

## The Accounting Equation

**Assets = Liabilities + Owner's Equity**

Every transaction maintains this balance. The equation can also be written as:

**Assets = Liabilities + Capital + Revenue - Expenses - Drawings**

## Journal Entry Verification Checklist

1. ✅ Total Debits = Total Credits
2. ✅ Account types identified correctly
3. ✅ Amounts match supporting documents
4. ✅ Date is correct
5. ✅ Narration describes the transaction
6. ✅ VAT/TDS considered if applicable

*These are standard accounting rules applicable in Nepal. For complex transactions or specific industry requirements, consult a Chartered Accountant.*
